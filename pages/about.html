A country town kid with a love for TECH!

I grew up and currently reside in a regional town in the Wheatbelt of Western Australia.

I have worked in several industries since graduating from high school in 2008, including retail, meat processing, and the medical industry.

In 2012, I successfully completed my Certificate III in Meat Processing and my Certificate IV in Meat Safety as a Meat Inspector.

In 2016, I switched careers to the medical industry, starting as a support services worker before transitioning to administrative roles.

In 2022, I began learning a skill I never had the opportunity to explore growing up in the country - PROGRAMMING!

A little bit of a NERD!

I think it goes without saying, that if you want to study programming, you likely have nerdy interests.

I enjoy painting warhammer, playing table-top rpg's with my friends, and am very much looking forward to gaming again once my studies are completed.

I have a large collection of various consoles and digital media, as well as boardgames and books.

I like old school RPG's like the original Baulders Gate 1 & 2, Arcanum: Of Magick and Steamworks Obscura and Fallout 1 & 2

Additionally, I have successfully run to completion several campagins for Dungeons and Dragons and Call of Cthuhlu.

A fiend for the DARKER things.

I am an all out devoted enthusiast to all things HORROR.

There is something fascinating about the macabre and what makes really gets to people. In addition to my love for horror media, I am also a Metal Head.

Although I listen to a wide variety of music, my favourite artists of all time are the following: Deathspell Omega, Spawn of Possession and Necrophagist.

My favourite movie of all time is John Carpenters "The Thing".

I also enjoy several horror authors including the infamous H.P Lovecraft, Laird Barron and the legendary Stephen King.

One day, I hope to be able to create my own horror project through various art forms such as Games, Short Films and Literature.
